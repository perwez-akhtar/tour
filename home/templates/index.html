Delhi Agra and Jaipur Golden Triangle Tour

    - In the morning between 9:00 -11 am (Pick up from Delhi Hotel and Airport), our representative along with driver takes you for the Delhi Agra Jaipur Tour Package. Initially, we will visit you to the Red Fort, situated in the old Delhi area. After that, we will take you to the Humanyun Tomb, his 15th-century building was a perfect example of a splendid architecture of that time. (Humanyun Tomb visit only on Monday) Later, we will drive you to the attractively constructed Lotus temple in the shape of the half-open lotus flower. (closed on Monday if you doing this tour on day then out site visit only)
    - Next morning check out from the hotel, our driver will pick you up around 7:00 am from your Delhi hotel and drive you to the Agra for around 4-5 hours journey for the further journey of your Delhi Agra and Jaipur Golden Triangle Tour. On arrival at Agra Check into the Hotel, after freshening up, our expert guide will join you for your Agra excursion and proceeds you to the globally famous Taj Mahal. After Lunch, our next move is towards the Agra Fort also remarked as the treasure house of Mughals. The fort houses so many buildings include Diwan-i-Am, Nagina Masjid, Diwan-i-Khas and much more.
    - In the next Morning after breakfast and check out from the Hotel, our chauffeur will take you to visit the Fatehpur Sikri, renowned for the tomb of Saint Sheikh Salim Chisti. On arrival to Jaipur check in to the Hotel, after rest and fresh up, our driver will take you to the visit to Birla Temple and drop you back to the hotel for overnight stay.
    - In the morning after breakfast and check out from the hotel, our driver along with the expert guide will take you to the Amber Fort, a perfect example of Rajput and Muslim style of architecture. You can also enjoy an elephant ride here in Amber Fort. After that, you will be driven towards the Jal Mahal, placed in a midst of a Man Sagar lake. The panoramic view of the Palace makes it the most romantic place of Jaipur. Here, you can have fun on Camel. Afterward, our next move is towards the Hawa Mahal, the most iconic place of Jaipur.
